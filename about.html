About this App:

Its Origin

The idea came from a pool of ideas from a brainstorm session. Here's a little sneak to that session: "A project topic that resonates with me a lot is: Music. It's always been a huge part of my life (to get through the hard times or to just jam out). So I would love to give back, and create an app relating to this. Some other topics I would like to explore are Food, Maps, and Travelling. I really enjoy trying out new types of food and love travelling to explore different cultures and scenaries."

What Does It Do?

It is essentially an app that allows a group of people to listen to the same music playlist in real time, altogether and synchronized. It would be "useful as" it could emulate a private dj party with just your smartphone, the app, your friends, and headphones of your choosing. The idea is to have the ability to have a mini party wherever you are (on campus, at Gasworks, in Paris, NYC, Italy, Korea, etc.)

The Finished Product

Upon the completion of the app, it will allow you to host a DJ session in real-time. The only things that everyone will need are a: charged phone and headphones (the nicer they are, the better the experience will be). The goal is to have an immersed, fun, and epic experience with your friends or even meet new people that have similar music interests!

Its Features

It will also have a quick navigation of what kind of music you would like to play. It would offer 5 popular genres, 5 different moods, and the top 5 popular songs at that moment. It will also have the ability to search a single song or an album! This would hopefully make it seamless to discover new music as well. The Music Player section will have the typical play, rewind, and forward buttons. And, the album cover will of course be displayed and change when the song's album is different.
